DP x16

World's Most Innovative VST

DP x16 is a cutting-edge VST (Virtual Studio Technology) plugin designed for modern music producers, sound designers, and audio engineers seeking to push the boundaries of creative sound manipulation. A versatile powerhouse, it blends classic analog warmth with futuristic digital processing, delivering an expansive range of dynamic and complex textures.

16-Slot Signal Chain

The DP x16 features a unique 16-slot signal chain, where each slot represents an individual processing module. Users can drag and drop from a comprehensive selection of effects (filters, EQs, modulation, delays, reverbs, distortion, and more), allowing for full customization of their sound.

Modular Routing

The routing system offers complete freedom, letting users connect modules in series, parallel, or even in hybrid configurations. Advanced signal path editing tools make it easy to experiment with complex signal flows.

Dynamic Processing Engine

The core of DP x16 is its dynamic processing engine, which adapts to incoming audio material, offering real-time spectral analysis and adaptive gain staging. This engine gives users total control over dynamics without sacrificing quality or introducing distortion.

Multi-Layered FX Units

A combination of traditional effects (reverb, delay, chorus, etc.) and innovative new processors (fractals, spectral resonators, pitch shifters, granular filters, etc.) can be layered within the 16-slot chain for truly intricate processing.
